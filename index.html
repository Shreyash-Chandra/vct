VCT EXPLAINED IN EASY TERMS!

    Format:

  • VCT Americas
  • VCT EMEA
  • VCT Pacific
  • VCT China

Games has partnerships with 10 eSports organizations in each of the 4 regions above, adding 1 extra team on top of the 10 teams each year via ascension games. The winner of ascension gets to play VCT with the franchised teams.
The franchised teams then play each other in their respective regions to win a limited number of spots in the Masters, which is the 2nd biggest VCT event after Champions. VCT Masters are held in different countries each year and the top 3 teams from every region get a spot in the masters to play with teams from other regions and decide which region is better. The winning team gets their region an extra slot for the next event that's going to happen.
Champs is the biggest event that is held every year at the very end and it's the end of that year's VCT. The winner of the Champs is considered the best team in the world until next year.
Four teams from each region participate in championships. Three of them are the teams with the highest points in their region, and one team qualifies by winning the "Last Chance Qualifier" event which is held 2-3 weeks prior to champs and the winner from each region qualifies for champs.
