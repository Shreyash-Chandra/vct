Gambit were for certain the strongest team in EMEA if not the world. They were valorant's 2nd masters winners defeating Envy(optic later) 3-0 in the grand finals.
Due to all the Russia vs Ukraine thing and Gambit being a Russian org, they had to disband. The players later join different orgs. 
Their lead players Chronical and nAts joining Fnatic and Team Liquid respectively. Fnatic with the addition of Chronical won 2 events back to back in 2023 making them the only team to have 2 trophies
and nAts joining Liquid making them EMEA's champions in 2023. Although Liquid couldn't perform upto expectations in 2023, they're back in 2024 to show what they're capable of.
